Film Producer

Job Description - A Film Producer is a person who oversees film production.

  • Salary Info - They make an average of $68,440 per year.
  • Job Availability - This is a less competitive job. A person can produce a film on their own and be self-employed.
  • Education Requirement - A typical film producer has a film production degree or has taken a class is needed.
