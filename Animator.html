Animator

Job Description - An Animator is a person who makes animated films, shows apps and games mainly for entertainment.

  • Salary Info - An animator makes an average of $50,000 per year.
  • Job Availability - There are a lot of jobs in this field, and a person can work for themselves.
  • Education Requirement - An undergraduate degree in any animation or arts courses is necessary
