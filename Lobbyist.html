Lobbyist

Job Description - A Lobbyist is an activist who persuades members of the government to enact legislation that would benefit their group.

  • Salary Info - A lobbyist makes an average of $44.3k - $130k per year.
  • Job Availability - This is a competitive field.
  • Education Requirement - A degree in politics, knowledge of the government and experience is necessary.
